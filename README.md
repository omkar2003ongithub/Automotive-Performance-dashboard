**Automotive Sales & Service Performance Dashboard.**

📊 **Project Overview**:
This project focuses on transforming raw transactional automotive data into actionable business intelligence. Using IBM Cognos Analytics and Microsoft Excel, I developed a comprehensive two-page dashboard to monitor regional dealership performance, sales trends, and vehicle reliability metrics.

The goal of this analysis was to provide stakeholders with a clear view of profitability and service efficiency, enabling data-driven decision-making for inventory management and customer service improvements.

**🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills**
Business Intelligence: IBM Cognos Analytics, Looker

Data Processing: Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, Power Query, Advanced Formulas)

Data Visualization: KPI Design, Heatmaps, Treemaps, Multi-axis Trend Charts

Analytics: Trend Analysis, Sentiment Analysis, Root Cause Analysis

**🚀 Key Features & Dashboards**
1. Sales Performance Dashboard
Designed to track high-level financial health and regional sales distribution.

Executive KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Profit (formatted in Millions), Total Quantity Sold, and Average Order Value.

Dealer Performance: A clustered column chart analyzing profit across 10+ territories, sorted to identify top-performing and underperforming dealers.

Product Demand: Visualization of sales volume by vehicle model to identify market preferences.

2. Service & Reliability Dashboard
Focused on post-sale metrics and customer satisfaction.

Recall Heatmap: A data-density map correlating vehicle models with specific affected systems (e.g., Brakes, Engine, Electronics) to streamline technical root-cause analysis.

Customer Sentiment: A treemap visualization comparing positive, neutral, and negative customer feedback to gauge brand health.

Profit vs. Volume Correlation: A dual-axis line and column chart comparing monthly units sold against net profit to identify seasonal margin fluctuations.

**💡 Business Insights**
Profitability Gaps: Identified specific dealer territories with high sales volume but lower-than-average profit margins, suggesting a need for price optimization.

Reliability Patterns: Discovered a high frequency of electronic-system recalls in specific models, allowing for proactive service planning.

Seasonal Trends: Correlated profit spikes with specific months, aiding in more accurate quarterly forecasting.
